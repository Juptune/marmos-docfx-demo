Module - std.complex

About

This module contains the $(LREF Complex) type, which is used to represent complex numbers, along with related mathematical operations and functions.

$(LREF Complex) will eventually $(DDLINK deprecate, Deprecated Features, replace) the built-in types cfloat , cdouble , creal , ifloat , idouble , and ireal .
