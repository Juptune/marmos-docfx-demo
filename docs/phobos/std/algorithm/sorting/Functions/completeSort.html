void completeSort(
  alias less = "a < b",
  SwapStrategy ss = SwapStrategy.unstable,
  Lhs,
  Rhs
)(
  SortedRange!(Lhs,less) lhs,
  Rhs rhs
)

Sorts the random-access range chain(lhs, rhs) according to predicate less .

The left-hand side of the range lhs is assumed to be already sorted; rhs is assumed to be unsorted. The exact strategy chosen depends on the relative sizes of lhs and rhs . Performs $(BIGOH lhs.length + rhs.length log(rhs.length)) (best case) to $(BIGOH (lhs.length + rhs.length) log(lhs.length + rhs.length)) (worst-case) evaluations of $(REF_ALTTEXT swap, swap, std,algorithm,mutation).
