About

Represents a sorted range. In addition to the regular range primitives, supports additional operations that take advantage of the ordering, such as merge and binary search. To obtain a $(D SortedRange) from an unsorted range r , use $(REF sort, std,algorithm,sorting) which sorts r in place and returns the corresponding SortedRange . To construct a SortedRange from a range r that is known to be already sorted, use $(LREF assumeSorted).

Params

pred: The predicate used to define the sortedness opt: Controls how strongly the range is checked for sortedness. Will only be used for RandomAccessRanges . Will not be used in CTFE.
