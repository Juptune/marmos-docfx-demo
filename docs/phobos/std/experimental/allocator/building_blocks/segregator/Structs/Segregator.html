About

Dispatches allocations (and deallocations) between two allocators ($(D SmallAllocator) and LargeAllocator ) depending on the size allocated, as follows. All allocations smaller than or equal to threshold will be dispatched to SmallAllocator . The others will go to LargeAllocator .

If both allocators are shared , the Segregator will also offer $(D shared) methods.
