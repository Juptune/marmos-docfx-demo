About

A Bucketizer uses distinct allocators for handling allocations of sizes in the intervals $(D [min, min + step - 1]), $(D [min + step, min + 2 step - 1]), $(D [min + 2 **step, min + 3 ** step - 1]), ... , $(D [max - step + 1, max]).

Bucketizer holds a fixed-size array of allocators and dispatches calls to them appropriately. The size of the array is $(D (max + 1 - min) / step), which must be an exact division.

Allocations for sizes smaller than min or larger than max are illegal for Bucketizer . To handle them separately, Segregator may be of use.
