About

A dynamic array of bits. Each bit in a BitArray can be manipulated individually or by the standard bitwise operators & , | , ^ , ~ , >> , << and also by other effective member functions; most of them work relative to the BitArray 's dimension (see $(LREF dim)), instead of its $(LREF length).
